About Me

During my time at the university of Maribor in Slovenia, Photography started as a hobby.I quickly developed a passion for capturing the beauty of cars through my camera lens. As a car enthusiast myself, I understand the importance of capturing every detail of a vehicle to showcase its unique features and character.

I approach each project with a keen eye for detail and a desire to tell a story. Whether I'm shooting for a car dealership, a private seller, or a car enthusiast, I take the time to understand the client's needs and tailor my approach to capture images that exceed their expectations.

In addition to my technical skills and creative vision, I also pride myself on my professionalism and client-focused approach. I believe in building strong relationships with my clients and working closely with them to ensure that their vision is realized in the final images.

I'm excited to share my portfolio with you and invite you to take a look at some of my past work! If you're interested in working with me or have any questions about my services, please don't hesitate to reach out.
